Desserts

Nero Belgian Waffle Bar
1703 Robson St, Vancouver, BC V6G 1C8
Nero prides themselves on making everything in-house and from scratch using the freshest natural and local ingredients, complemented with specialty ingredients from Belgium. They serve Brussels and Liege waffles with a full menu of toppings including sweet and savoury options.

Rocky Point Ice Cream
2800 Murray St, Port Moody, BC V3H 1X2
Rocky Point Ice Cream started out in 1997 as a summer ice cream shop operating out of a tiny kiosk. Their ice cream was provided by another small batch operator in Vancouver and has grown into new flavours. Follow their Instagram here.


Earnest Ice Cream
1829 Quebec Street, Vancouver, BC
Earnest Ice Cream was born from friendship and a shared love of ice cream. Since they launched in 2012, their goal has been to create ice cream that expresses their passion for flavour.
